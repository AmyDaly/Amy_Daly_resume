Hello there

My Name is Amy and I am an aspiring Software Engineer. I started out my career as an Optometrist in 2012. I have an Optometry degree that I obtained at the University of Johannesburg, but I have always had an interest in Tech and decided to pursue this at the tender age of 34. I enrolled in the HyperionDev Software Engineering Bootcamp in July, and it has been an amazing learning experience. In the future I would love to work in an environment where I could use both my knowledge and skills from Optometry together with the skills I have learned in Software Development. I enjoy a good challenge and thrive on learning. On the personal side of things I enjoy the outdoors and spending time with my family.

I have 11 years experience in Optometry, six of which I worked in a clinical environment within Ophthalmology. After having children of my own I developed a great interest in paediatric and behavioral optometry, and have since worked within this specialised area of optometry ensuring quality care for children and neuro-optometric patients. I would love to be able to bring resources to assist patients with their care through the implementation of Software.
